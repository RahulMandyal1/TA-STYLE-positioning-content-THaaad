Use Fullness of cats

in our aurban and surbuan house what should be do without cats? in our sitting bedrooms, our libraries, in our kitchen ,our storerooms, our farms ,barns,rickyards,in our docks, our granaries,our sheeps ,our wraves,in our corn markets, meat markets and other places too neumerous to mention how useful they are! in our ships. the rats oft sets them at defilance still they are of great service.


How wonderfully patient is the cat when watching for rats or mice, awaiting their egress from their place of refuge or that which is their home! How well Shakespeare in Pericles, Act ill., describes this keen attention of the cat to its natural pursuit!
